VodKa

Vodka is a type of distilled spirit, with "Vodka" deriving from the Latin term meaning "water of life.

Gin

Gin is made from juniper berries, and the term "Gin" is derived from the French pronunciation of "genièvre," which means juniper.


Whisky

Whiskey is primarily made from ingredients like barley, malted barley, rye, and corn. After fermentation and distillation, it matures in wooden casks. Various regions have differences in their choice of ingredients.


Tequila

The most well-known agave spirit is undoubtedly Tequila, often regarded as the soul of Mexico. Generally, Tequila carries a citrusy and sweet aroma, a characteristic attributed to the agave plant.


Rum

Rum was originally created using the residual molasses from sugar refining, which, after dilution with water, was fermented with the addition of yeast.

Brandy

When we refer to "Cognac" in general, we are talking about a distilled spirit made from wine, hence carrying the fruity aroma of grapes.
